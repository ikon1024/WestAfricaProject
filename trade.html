1: Geography & Trade

Africa has unique geographical features that seperate it from other continents. Africa is known for one of the most hottest deserts known as the Sahara. Its also one of the biggest deserts in the world as it spreads over 3.5 million miles. The Sahara desert. West Africa is a place with varied vegetation of desert, semidesert, and a savanna. In the south of the Sahara is a semidesert called the Sahel. Each of these areas affected trade.
The needs for items in diffrent places prompted for the establishment of trade. These varying climates in Africa affected trade and the demand for various goods in different regions.

The niger river is a river used for transportation and trade, the niger river also provided water.
